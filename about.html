Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a leading creative agency

2016

The Beginning

Brand was founded with a vision to create digital experiences that truly matter. Starting with just 3 people, we focused on crafting meaningful work for clients who shared our values.

2018

Growing Our Expertise

As our client base grew, so did our team and capabilities. We expanded our services to include brand strategy and digital marketing, allowing us to deliver more effective end-to-end solutions.

2020

International Recognition

Our work began to be recognized with multiple industry awards. We began working with international clients and established our reputation as a forward-thinking creative practice.

2023

Where We Are Today

Today, Brand is a team of 30+ creative thinkers and strategists dedicated to helping brands navigate the digital landscape. We continue to create, innovate, and push the boundaries of what's possible.
